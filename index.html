In/Different Spaces

Statement

In / different spaces is an exhibition of photographic work by 2nd Year BA Photography students from the University of Portsmouth.

The show’s title references a collection of essays by Victor Burgin. Within these essays Burgin draws from psychoanalysis, semiotics, art history and philosophy: proposing that, in relation to memory and visual culture, photographic images exist in spaces far more complex than simple representations of the world.

The exhibition seeks to suggest this complexity, both within the images themselves and through their relationships with each other. A deliberate avoidance of any conventional context is here set to work, by these artists, as a means to problematise any essay assumptions we may have about the images that surround us.
